About the Faculty

The Faculty of Physical Sciences at UDS Nyankpala Campus offers undergraduate and postgraduate programs, and engages in research and community service. Our mission is to train competent scientists and contribute to national development.

Vision

To be a leading center for science education and research in the region.

Mission

  • Deliver high-quality science education
  • Promote research and innovation
  • Engage with communities to apply science for development
